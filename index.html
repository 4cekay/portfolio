Hey there, I'm KC

Interactive Media & Game Design


I am in my 3rd year at Simon Fraser University, working towards my BSc in Interactive Arts & Technology (SIAT).
I've accumulated a diverse skillset in design and development, and am passionate about bridging the gap between artistic and technical sides to bring creative visions to life! I am especially interested in immersive experiences that engage audiences through interaction, choice, and storytelling.


Throughout my time at SFU, I've gotten to work on projects in areas ranging from game design, web and app development, to interactive art installations. I am also an avid gamer, musician, and artist, and love to explore potential intersections between interests in my endeavours.

My goal is to create memorable work that pushes the boundaries of my imagination!
